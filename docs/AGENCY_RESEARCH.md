### Key Copy Patterns Observed

1. **"System, not services"** -- Top agencies position themselves as building systems/frameworks, not selling hourly work. This is directly relevant to admate's "90 Dni do Systemu" section.

2. **Financial language over marketing jargon** -- Best agencies talk about profit, ROAS, contribution margin, unit economics, P&L -- not impressions and clicks. CTC, Structured, Kynship, Rise DTC all lead with financial outcomes.

3. **Anti-agency positioning** -- Many successful agencies explicitly distance themselves from the "traditional agency" stereotype. NoGood, Digitalk, Advertsy all use this effectively.

4. **Specificity wins** -- Concrete numbers beat vague promises. "96% channel growth in 90 days" (Stimulate), "$105M in email revenue" (Hustler), "9 out of 10 accounts we take over perform better" (Happy Parrots).

5. **Short, punchy taglines** -- The best lines are 3-7 words: "Love Growth. Hate Waste." / "Growth is in the Doing." / "Creative That Converts." / "Authenticity Wins."

6. **Customer-first framing** -- "Partners, Not Clients" (Lilo Social), "Your Outsourced CMO" (Hawke), "Focus on your business. We'll bring the customers." (Shoelace).

7. **From  To transformation** -- Several agencies use a before/after framing: "Z chaotycznych promocji do systematycznego wzrostu" (Growth Hub), "From Unknown to Known" (Retention.com), "Turn first-time buyers into lifelong customers" (Stimulate).

### Actionable Takeaways for admate.pl

1. **"90 Dni do Systemu"** -- Lean into the "system" language. Agencies like Structured and Kynship prove this resonates. Frame the 90-day journey as building a revenue machine, not "setting up campaigns."

2. **"Kreacje & Content"** -- Use the "Creative That Converts" / "Engineered for the feed" angle. Emphasize that creative isn't just pretty -- it's the biggest performance lever.

3. **"Platne Kampanie"** -- Lead with profit, not traffic. "Zamieniamy klikniecia w zlotowki" (Digitalk) is the gold standard for Polish performance copy.

4. **"Email Marketing"** -- "Turn first-time buyers into lifelong customers" and "Unleash the power of repeat revenue" are strongest retention hooks. Emphasize the 40-60x ROI of email vs paid.

5. **General tone** -- Be specific, be bold, avoid generic agency language. Use numbers. Use short sentences. Position as partner, not vendor.
