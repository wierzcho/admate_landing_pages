# ICP - Ideal Customer Profile

> **"My ICP is female ecommerce business owners between the ages of 30-50 who make between $200k and $1 million per year in revenue."**

**(50k-330k PLN/month revenue)**

---

## Section 1: The Ideal Client Avatar

- **Gender**: Female eCommerce brand owner
- **Age**: 30-50 years old
- **Revenue**: 50k-1M PLN/month
- **Ad spend**: 10k+ PLN/month on Meta Ads
- **Location**: Poland-based
- **Business stage**: Established business (not startup) with proven product-market fit
- **Team**: Small team (3+ people)

---

## Section 2: Pain Points & Frustrations

- Current agency uses junior staff who follow templates
- ROAS declining or stagnant despite increasing spend
- Can't scale profitably past current revenue ceiling
- Overwhelmed by constant Meta algorithm changes
- Feeling like "just another account" at their current agency
- No strategic partnership - just task execution
- Creative fatigue - same ads running too long
- Email marketing neglected or non-existent
- Losing 97% of traffic that doesn't convert first visit

---

## Section 3: Goals & Desires

- Wants expert-level, personal attention from senior specialists
- Needs someone who truly understands eCommerce (not generalist agency)
- Looking for a growth partner, not just an "ads manager"
- Values aesthetics and brand consistency in creatives
- Wants predictable, scalable revenue growth
- Desires CEO-level strategic conversations
- Wants to maximize every złoty spent on ads
- Looking for omnichannel approach (ads + email + retention)

---

## Section 4: Buying Triggers

- Agency contract renewal approaching
- Recent performance drop (ROAS tanking)
- Competitor visibly outperforming them
- Recommendation from trusted source / friend
- Saw Beata's content and resonated with her approach
- Black Friday / Q4 approaching and current setup won't scale
- Just raised prices and need to justify with better marketing
- New product launch needs expert promotion

---

## Section 5: Disqualifiers (Anti-ICP)

- **DIY mentality** → Send to Akademia Adsów instead
- **Price shoppers** → Looking for cheapest agency, not best
- **Under 50k PLN/month revenue** → Not enough margin to afford premium service
- **No proven product-market fit** → Still testing products, not ready to scale
- **Expecting instant miracles** → "I need 10x ROAS by next week"
- **Dropshipping / low-margin products** → Unit economics don't support ad spend
- **Doesn't value email marketing** → Won't implement full funnel approach
- **Control freaks** → Wants to approve every single ad variation

---
